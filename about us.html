Anime-51

Layout

Anime-51 is a movie theatre situated in Game City Shopping Center opposite CNA store on the 1st level.
Anime-51 acquires and distributes mainly animation movies from prominent international filmmakers that appeal to the interests and passion of all types of fans. Anime-51 is a fully integrated, next-generation entertainment studio with a fan-first philosophy anchored in the company’s omnichannel approach to its immersive offerings. From streaming and home entertainment to theatrical distribution and broadcast, ANIME-51’s commitment to character-driven storytelling, combined with tech innovation and key distribution partnerships, provides audiences with multiple and meaningful touchpoints to connect with this immersive world. Such is our mandate as a company.
