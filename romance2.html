KOMI CAN'T COMMUNICATE

RELEASE DATE:15 AUG 2022
WATCHING DATES:15.09.2022-19.09.2022
GENRE:ROMANCE
DURATION:128min
Though Shouko is regarded as the Madonna of her school, she is incapable of socializing with others to such a level that it can be called a communication disorder. She is unable to utter a word or sound in her daily life due to her crippling anxiety and fear of rejection by her peers.
